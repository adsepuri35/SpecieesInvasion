Spotted Lanternflies

The Spotted lanternfly has taken over North America and can even be seen in familiar places like the Rutgers campus. It causes serious damage to the surrounding, native plants.

Lionfish

Lionfish destroy coral reefs and marine ecosystems. They disrupt the food chain and commercial fishing in the Atlantic. See how they can be stopped!

Purple Loosestrife

Purple loosestrife negatively affects both wildlife and agriculture. It displaces and replaces native flora and fauna, eliminating food, nesting and shelter for wildlife.
