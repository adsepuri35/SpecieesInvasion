The Problem

Invasive species that have already established a prescense can be difficult to control or eradicate.

  • - Talk to your local nursery when selecting plants for gardens.

  • - Volunteer at species removal efforts.

  • - Only fish using native bait. Identify if bait is invasive.

  • - Clean your hiking and fishing gear.
